## Style Note

Warm and grounded, never decorative — a working engineer's notebook, not a mood board. Type: IBM Plex Serif / IBM Plex Sans. Colors: `#F1E9DA` bg, `#2B211A` text, `#8B6F4E` main, `#3E7C74` accent (used sparingly).

---

## Contrast Check

- Text (`#2B211A`) on background (`#F1E9DA`): **13.04:1** — passes WCAG AA for all text sizes.
- Accent (`#3E7C74`) and main (`#8B6F4E`) on background: **~4:1** — passes for large text/buttons/links only, not for small body paragraphs.

*Rule of thumb: main and accent colors are for headings, buttons, and links — never for paragraph text.*
