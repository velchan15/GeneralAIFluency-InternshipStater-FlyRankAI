# ai-fluency-week01-portfolio-proof

Week 1 proof statement for FlyRank AI Fluency track — defining the core claim, audience, and action for my portfolio.

**Proof Statement:** 

I can turn a real problem I've encountered into a working IoT device — proven through PureStream, a real-time water quality monitoring system built on ESP32 that I designed after my own family experienced health issues from drinking water whose quality was never actually monitored. This is aimed at R&D hiring managers at IoT startups such as PT Nocola IoT Solution or PT LEN Innovation Technology, with the goal of getting them to download my CV, which includes my contact information.

**Why this needs to exist:** 

My CV and LinkedIn can only state a single line like "built an IoT water quality monitoring system," while this portfolio shows the actual thinking process, testing data, and technical problems I solved — the things that actually prove my ability, not just claim it.
